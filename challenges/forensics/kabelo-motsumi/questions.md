# Exercise Questions — Kabelo Motsumi

Work through phases in order. Each phase builds on the previous. New evidence is distributed by your instructor when you have established the prior forensic link.

---

## Phase 1 — Dumpster Dive and Blockchain Trace

*Starting point: physical trash from Kagiso Supplies.*

1. What Bitcoin address did you recover from the discarded material?
2. Using a public blockchain explorer, trace the transaction history for that address. Funds arriving here are periodically swept onward. What cryptocurrency exchange is the destination for those sweeps?
3. Outgoing transactions also route to a second address on a different exchange. What is that second exchange? What legal channel — not a direct request — would be required to obtain records from it given its jurisdiction?

---

## Phase 2 — Exchange Legal Return and Burner Account Baseline

*Your instructor will distribute the Luno legal process return and the `kabo-seretse-operational` search warrant return after Phase 1.*

4. What email address is registered to the Luno account linked to the BTC deposit address?
5. When was the Luno account created?
6. The Google SubscriberInfo login records for the burner account show a consistent IP pattern across all sessions — except one. What is the anomalous IP address? How does it differ from all the others?
7. After you have identified the anomalous IP, your instructor will distribute the ISP subscriber return. What full name does the subscriber record attribute to that IP address?

## Phase 3 — Burner Account Content and Account Correlation

*Continue in the `kabo-seretse-operational` search warrant return.*

9. The burner account's LinkedByCookies file records a second Google account that shared a browser session on the same physical device. What is that account's email address?

## Phase 4 — Coinbase Egmont Return

*Your instructor will distribute the Coinbase Egmont return at the start of this phase.*

11. What BTC receive address is registered to that Coinbase account?
12. BTC received at the Coinbase address was converted to USD and wired to a bank account. What are the last four digits of the destination account?

## Phase 5 — Personal Account: Identity and Login Records

*Your instructor will distribute the `kabelo-motsumi-personal` search warrant return after you have: (a) identified the real account via the burner's LinkedByCookies file; (b) obtained the ISP subscriber name; and (c) resolved the initials from the trash note against that name.*

14. What employer appears in the personal account's profile? Cross-reference this with the LinkedIn profile your instructor provides.
15. The personal account login records show a clear transition point. On what date do logins from the residential IP stop and VPN logins begin?
